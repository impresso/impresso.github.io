How can “transmedia” history be put into practice from both theoretical and empirical perspectives? The international conference [“Transmedia History”](https://impresso.github.io/transmedia/en)—organised by the Impresso project and the [University of Lausanne’s History Department](https://www.unil.ch/hist/home.html)—will gather scholars from various backgrounds around this question to exchange views on new prospects opened by digitisation and digital tools to carry out transmedia research.
